Banku recipe

Fufu is a popular West African dish, particularly common in Ghana, Nigeria, and other parts of the region. It's a starchy side dish with a dough-like consistency, often served as a complement to soups and stews.

Fufu is known for its unique texture - soft and slightly elastic - and is typically eaten by pinching off small portions with your fingers and dipping it into accompanying sauces or soups.

Ingredients

  • Cassava
  • Water
  • Plantain flour
